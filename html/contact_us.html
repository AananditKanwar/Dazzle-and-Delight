Frequently Asked
Questions

What are your shipping options and costs?

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days) and express shipping (1-2 business days). Standard shipping is free for orders over $50, otherwise it's $4.99. Express shipping is $9.99 for all orders. Shipping costs and estimated delivery times are displayed at checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also log into your account and view your order status under "My Orders.

What is your return and exchange policy?

We offer free returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags attached. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days of receiving the return.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location and are calculated at checkout.

How does your loyalty program work?

Our loyalty program rewards you with points for every purchase. You earn 1 point for every $1 spent. Points can be redeemed for discounts on future purchases. You also get exclusive access to members-only sales and early access to new collections.

Are there any ongoing sales or promotions?

Check our "Sale" section for current discounts. We also run seasonal promotions and flash sales. Sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on the latest deals.
